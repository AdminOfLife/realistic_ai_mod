The Sony Playstation 3 Platform

Discussion

Bink now runs on Sony's new Playstation 3 game console! The Sony PS3 is fast and has a great GPU - you'll be able to make play beautiful videos on it. Most developers will ship with HD 1280x720 videos on the PS3.

We did a lot of optimizations for this platform. The PS3 used an interesting blend of our PPC assembly, Linux, and OpenGL experience. The SPUs are especially tricky to get working, but once you do - wow! Bink can decompress up to ten 1280x720 or thirty-six 640x480 videos simultaneously on the PS3! This is by far our fastest platform!

The GPU also took quite a bit of work to tame, but once we did, the fragment shaders make video look wonderful!

Bink is extremely easy to use and integrate under this platform. This chapter will discuss everything you need to know!
