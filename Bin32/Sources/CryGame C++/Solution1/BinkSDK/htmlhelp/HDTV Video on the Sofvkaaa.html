HDTV Video on the Sony PS3

Discussion

Using Bink's pixel shader method of video playback, the Sony PS3 can easily play gorgeous 1280x720 or 1920x1080 video. Most developers will ship with cutscenes at 1280x720, so Bink has you covered. This will let your game cut-scenes look much, much better than DVD when played on HDTV displays!

To do this, compress your movies at a resolution of 1280x720, with a data rate of around 1,500,000 bytes per second. This will give you great looking video that the Sony PS3 will play without breaking a sweat.

Video on the Sony PS3 will really look amazing - we're excited to see what you all come up with!
