The Win32 Platform

Discussion

Bink was first developed on the Win32 platform. Win32 is pretty optimal to develop under now-a-days, almost all of our other platforms are actually built on Win32.

Bink now supplies background thread decompression on Win32. Unless you intend to support Windows 95 or 98, we recommend everyone switch to threaded playback, even on single core CPUs. It will provide much smoother playback.

Bink is extremely easy to use and integrate under this platform. This chapter will discuss everything you need to know!
